communication, telemetry dashboards, C#/.NET and Python, embedded Linux

project (motor controllers, FOC, ODrive-style hardware, mechanical
design). Owns a 3D printer and cares about *practical, useful prints*
and printing technique far more than industrial 3D-printing market news.

Strong interests: embedded systems and microcontrollers (ESP32, STM32),
robotics, motor control, new SBCs and chips, AI models and tooling
(especially local/open models and developer tools), clever engineering
write-ups and reverse-engineering stories.

Moderate interests: startup/company news when genuinely novel (not
routine funding rounds), space, world events with real consequences,
Netherlands news (lives/works in NL context), Turkey-relevant economics
(EUR/TRY), markets big-picture.

Low interest: celebrity/politics horse-race coverage, sports, corporate
press releases, market reports full of dollar figures, sponsored
content, industrial titanium-powder announcements.

Taste: prefers substance over hype; a good technical blog post beats a
press release; "how it works" beats "who raised money".
